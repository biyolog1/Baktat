Who we are

  • About
  • Our mission
  • Our vision

Chicken Good is a 100% American, family-owned and operated company, providing fresh shell eggs and egg products to supermarkets, restaurants and food manufacturers.

Our mission is to provide our community and neighbors with proven poultry and the patterns essential to developing sustainable poultry flocks.

Our vision, as purveyors of productive poultry, is to breed, grow and market locally-adapted breeds that are selected based on worldwide accepted poultry standards.

How we achieved success

Our History

Establishment

Our poultry farm was established in 1999 with a goal of providing high quality organic chicken and eggs to the local community.

First Success

In 2003, our farm was awarded the “Best Small Business” prize. We also introduced new poultry and extended our farm’s territory.

New Technologies

Four years later, our farm was updated with new innovative equipment that allowed us to improve the poultry feeding process.

National Recognition

A few years ago we gained national recognition when our farm joined the list of the most successful American organic farms.

Partnership

Last year we started our partnership with big distribution chains, which allowed us to introduce our products to more customers.
